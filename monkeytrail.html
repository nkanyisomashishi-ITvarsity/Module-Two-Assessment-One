ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

Where there are simians, follow the blue wooden path that will lead you to where a diverse amount of apes, monkeys, chimpanzees, gorillas, along with other simians dwell. The Monkey Trail has many ape-like creatures to observe and gaze upon carefully while on the wooden path.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail begins from the entrance of the zoo, all the way to the other side of the zoo, by the Dine @ The Zoo restaurant.
