LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest is a section of the zoo that contains a large variety of wild animals, mostly herbivores that are seperately allocated throughout the forest as to avoid accidents and interactions between the other animals. Here, the giraffes, koalas, monkeys and other herbivorous animals are going to be situated here.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?
